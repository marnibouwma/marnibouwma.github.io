The Landlord Special

This project explores the concept of aesthetic and functional compromises made in rental spaces. Through a mix of digital and traditional media, I highlight the makeshift repairs, mismatched paint jobs, and unique 'solutions' that define many rental properties.

By showcasing these details, I invite the viewer to reflect on the nature of temporary living and the improvisational ways tenants and landlords interact with space.
