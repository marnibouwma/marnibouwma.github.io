Charcoal drawings are my current passion, but I also love to paint and make digital drawings. Primarily, I love to draw portraits, animals, and plants. I have also ventured into making abstract art, dealing with themes such as anxiety and depression. The last category that I consider part of my practice is my imaginary topographical maps, which I value as a way of dealing with my mental illnesses. I love to encourage others to try this out as a coping mechanism too. One of my main passions as an artist is helping others to realize their love of and abilities to do art. I think that it is so important to have creative outlets and to make beautiful things to surround ourselves with.
